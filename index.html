Our Story

Food that tells
a story

Born from a love of Mediterranean heritage and the British countryside, Verdure was founded by Chef Amara Solis in 2018 with one belief: that the best food starts long before the kitchen.

The name means "greens" in Italian — a nod to our devotion to vegetables, herbs, and the living landscape that inspires every dish we create.
